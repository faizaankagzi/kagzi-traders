Our Story

Surat's Neighbourhood
Stationery Expert

Founded in 1995, Kagzi Traders has grown from a small neighbourhood shop to one of Muglisara's most trusted stationery and book stores. We serve students, professionals, and corporates across Surat with quality products and personalised service.

Owned by Mr. Zahid Kagzi, our shop carries an extensive range of writing instruments, notebooks, office supplies, books, and educational materials — all under one roof.
